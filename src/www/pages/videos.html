American Association for the Advancement of Science

Statement on climate change from 18 scientific associations

"Observations throught the world make it clear that climate change is occuring, and rigorous scientific research demonstrates that the greenhouse gases emitted by human activities are the primary driver. (2009)"

American Geophysical Union

"Human-induced climate change requires urgent action. Humanity is the major influence on the global climate change observed over the past 50 years. Rapid societal responses can significantly lessen negative outcomes. (Adopted 2003, revised and reaffirmed 2007, 2012, 2013)"
